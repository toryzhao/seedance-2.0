# Appendix F: Korean Cinematic & Photography Vocabulary

This reference provides standardized Korean terminology for precise communication with the Seedance 2.0 model, which often responds well to native technical terms.

---

## F.1 경별 (Shot Sizes)

| Term (KO) | Reading | English Equivalent | Notes |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **익스트림 롱 샷** | Ikseuteurim Rong Syat | Extreme Long Shot (ELS) | Focus on environment/scale |
| **롱 샷** | Rong Syat | Long Shot (LS) | Full body + environment |
| **풀 샷** | Pul Syat | Full Shot (FS) | Head to toe |
| **니 샷** | Ni Syat | Knee Shot | From knees up |
| **웨이스트 샷** | Weiseuteu Syat | Waist Shot | From waist up |
| **바스트 샷** | Baseuteu Syat | Bust Shot | From chest up |
| **클로즈업** | Keullojeueop | Close-Up (CU) | Face focus |
| **익스트림 클로즈업** | Ikseuteurim Keullojeueop | Extreme Close-Up (ECU) | Detail focus (eyes, hands) |

|:---|:---|:---|:---|

| **줌** | Jum | Zoom | Changing focal length |

## F.6 Direct-use Korean Phrases

- **「실사 영화 같은 질감」** (Texture like a live-action movie)
- **「얕은 피사체 심도」** (Shallow depth of field)
- **「역광으로 실루엣 강조」** (Emphasize silhouette with backlighting)
- **「슬로우 모션으로 극적인 연출」** (Dramatically in slow motion)
- **「핸드헬드 카메라의 현장감」** (Realism of a handheld camera)
